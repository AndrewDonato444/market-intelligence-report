# Persona: The Legacy Agent in Transition

## Profile

- **Name**: Pat Donovan
- **Age**: 60
- **Location**: Palm Beach
- **Experience**: 30 years, $500M+ in career sales
- **Market**: $10M+ waterfront estates
- **Practice**: Semi-retiring, mentoring junior agents, high-touch advisory

## Context

Pat has built a legacy career on relationships, but traditional networking is waning as younger clients prioritize data over personal connections. Pat's narrative-heavy updates lack the transparency of confidence ratings or proprietary scoring, risking irrelevance. Pat is also mentoring the next generation and needs tools they can hand off.

## Vocabulary

- Speaks in narratives — "what the market is telling us" (storyteller)
- Uses "advisory orbit" — maintains long-term client relationships
- Calls the report a "publication" or "intelligence brief"
- References "wealth managers" and "family offices" as partners
- Says "consultative legacy" — building something that outlasts them

## Patience Level

**High** — Pat values quality and will invest time to get the report right. Not in a rush. Appreciates editorial craft and narrative flow. Will want to read and refine every section. May be less comfortable with complex UI but deeply engaged with the content.

## Frustrations

- Younger clients who want data before they want relationships
- Narrative insights losing ground to quantified analysis
- No easy way to modernize without overhauling operations
- Risk of irrelevance in an intelligence-driven era
- Junior agents who lack the tools to carry on the advisory approach

## Success Metric

"Am I still the first call my clients make — and are my mentees equipped to be the next?"

## Why They Buy

The LMIR lets Pat modernize without overhauling operations. Liquidity Strength dimensions and editorial voice blend personal insights with data-backed positions. The architecture empowers mentees with decision frameworks and forecast transparency.

## Key Report Sections They Value

- Executive Summary (the narrative core — Pat's strength)
- Strategic Summary with Confidence Levels (modernizes Pat's instincts)
- Forward Outlook (advisory tool for long-term clients)
- Methodology & Sources (credibility for younger, data-hungry clients)
